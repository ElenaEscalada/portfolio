Redesign PC Squeeze Fruit Snack

Description

President’s Choice (PC), is a Canadian brand owned by Loblaw Companies Limited. Their motto is great quality with great value. Their squeeze fruit snacks punches contain a blend of puréed fruits, which is perfect for families-on-the-go worldwide.

I have chosen this product to redesign because as a mom PC fruit punches represents the perfect balance of healthy eating and portable convenience, but as a designer “Squeeze fruit snacks” is not a successful food-packaging design missing the opportunity to catch the consumer’s attention for different aspects..

have to two main goals to improve the packaging of this product: to appeal to kids’ sense of fun and reassure adults about squeeze fruit snacks as a healthy option. In other words, create a design where nutrition and fun go together in one colourful, creative and appetizing package that appeals to both parents and kids.

General design:

  1. Fan and unique package that connects with kids’ interest and imagination
  2. Simplify layout
  3. Two typeface combinations
  4. Pouch corners are rounded for safety
  5. Pouch’s cap: design is fun and easy for kids to open
Designed by Elena Escalada

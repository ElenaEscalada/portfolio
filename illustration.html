MMIW Postcard

Description

Client: College project
Software: Adobe Illustrator & Adobe InDesign

Postcard illustration and layout for the “No more stolen sisters” campaign to educate people about the actual dilemma of missing and murdered indigenous women and girls in Canada. This illustration shows indigenous women and girls are worthy and deserve to live, be protected and thrive like any woman or girl in the world.

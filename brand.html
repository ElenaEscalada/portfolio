Pride Toronto Logo Redesign

Description

The rebranding of Pride Toronto logo is a refresh of the original logo. A refresh designed to better reflect the 2SLGBTQ+ communities with a modern approach. My logo is the combination of an modern wordmark with the icon. The icon is a representation of human’s dna, which is the connection between all human beings. All human beings are valid and deserving of respect and love. This new icon creates a more dynamic image and adds a sense of meaning to the symbol. The typography is now set in one line with the emphasis on the word “TO”, diminutive of Toronto. Also, using a modern and pure geometry typeface, Cera Pro. The colours are the same than the previous logo closely associated with a rainbow, symbol of 2SLGBTQ+ communities.

Designed by Elena Escalada

DogDoc App

Description

Client: College project
Software: Adobe XD

DogDoc is a prototype app that aims to help dog owners carry all their dog information in one handy all-in-one app.

As the name suggests, this all-in-one dog app will provide dog owners with documentation and services in the same app. Dog lovers can view all their dog's data, such as health, and expenses, and provide browse, search, and map options to find canine friends, restaurants, parks, accommodations, and more, quickly and easily accessible.
